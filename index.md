# Shuqi Yang
**Email**: shuqi.yang.98@gmail.com

## About Me
I am a Software Engineer at Meta. My current work focuses on large-scale training performance optimization, including distributed training, kernel development, and quantization. Over the past 2.5 years, I have been working on the PyTorch Training Enablement team. Prior to that, I spent 2 years on Stream Processing infrastructure.

Before joining industry, I received my M.S. from Dartmouth College, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. [Bo Zhu](https://www.cs.dartmouth.edu/~bozhu/). My research focused on computer graphics, physical simulation, and machine learning. Before Dartmouth, I earned my B.Eng. degree from Dalian University of Technology.
